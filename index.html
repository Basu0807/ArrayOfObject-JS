Q-7 This time we are required to print the total marks of the students with marks greater than 50 after a grace of 15 marks has been added to those students who scored less than 50. 

        let totalMarks = studentRecords
        .map((student) => {
          if (student.marks < 50) {
            student.marks += 15;
          }
          return student;
        })
        .filter((student) => student.marks > 50)
        .reduce((sum, student) => sum + student.marks, 0)
        
        console.log('Total marks of students with marks greater than 50:', totalMarks);
    
Output:
        188

The map method is used to iterate over each student record and modify the marks if necessary by adding 15 to those who scored less than 50. The updated array is returned. The filter method is then used to filter out the students whose marks are still less than or equal to 50. Finally, the reduce method is used to sum up the marks of the filtered students, starting from an initial value of 0.
